# About Hiring, Partnerships, and Contributing

## Hiring

At the moment, [I'm](https://github.com/wceolin) working solo on this project, trying to bootstrap it as much as I can. Therefore, I don't have any budget for hiring.

## Contributing

The project is completely open-source and transparent. All contributions are welcome, and all communication is done on GitHub. Feel free to check out the org's profile, especially the [how we work section](https://github.com/zoonk/.github/blob/main/how-we-work/communication.md).

All information about the project is there. If you don't find an answer to your question, ask it on the [community forum](https://github.com/orgs/zoonk/discussions).

There are lots of moving parts, and things aren't very well organized yet as I work towards v1. You can see the [roadmap here on GitHub](https://github.com/zoonk/.github/blob/main/roadmap.md). I understand this isn't the best contributing experience so far, but things will get better over time.

If I eventually hire people to help out, I'll definitely favor those who contributed to the open-source project. However, keep in mind that contributing isn't a guarantee of future employment because, as I said, this is a small project for now, and my budget is very limited. I'm not even paying myself at the moment. 😅

Early interest is promising so far, but I have no idea if I'll succeed in turning this project into an actual business or not.

## Partnerships

Partnerships are also welcome. You can suggest a partnership on GitHub Discussions (preferred) or send an email to will@zoonk.org explaining what you have in mind and the expected outcome. Please be super objective and avoid marketing fluff.

## Questions

Feel free to [start a new discussion](https://github.com/orgs/zoonk/discussions/new/choose).
