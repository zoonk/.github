# Partnerships

Partnerships are welcome. You can suggest a partnership on [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/zoonk/discussions) (preferred) or send an email to will@zoonk.org explaining what you have in mind and the expected outcome. Please be super objective and avoid marketing fluff.
