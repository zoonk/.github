# Personas

## Organizations

### Manager / Team lead

Managers and team leads are responsible for overseeing projects and ensuring their teams are productive and motivated. They need tools for internal training, onboarding, and employee engagement.

### HR Representative

HR representatives handle hiring, employee relations, and compliance. They require effective onboarding processes and training materials to ensure smooth integration of new employees.

### Employee

Employees across various departments such as sales, marketing, legal, and engineering need continuous training and development. They benefit from interactive tutorials and step-by-step guides.

### Customer

They need effective customer support and tutorials to make the most out of the products our clients offer.

## Schools and universities

### Principal / Manager

Principals and managers oversee the administration of schools and universities. They need tools for student engagement, teacher training, and compliance with educational standards.

### Teacher

Teachers require interactive and engaging content to enhance student learning. They also need professional development resources and personalized learning paths for their students.

### Student

Students benefit from interactive and engaging content that makes learning fun and practical. They need personalized learning paths and data reports to track their progress.

### Parent

Parents are involved in their children's education. They need tools to engage with the learning process and support their children's educational journey.

## Individuals

### Content creator

Content creators can use Zoonk to sell courses online, build a brand, and generate additional revenue streams. They need tools to create and monetize their knowledge effectively.

### Small business owner

Small business owners can use Zoonk for internal training, onboarding, employee engagement or build their own online school easily. They need practical and hands-on learning tools.

### Student

Students, whether in formal education or self-learners, need high-quality, hands-on, and affordable education. They benefit from interactive courses and real-world problem-solving activities.
